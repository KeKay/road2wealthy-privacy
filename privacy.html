Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last updated / 最后更新：2025-12-14

English Version

Road2Wealthy is a financial education application designed to help users learn basic financial concepts, set financial goals, and track progress for educational purposes only.

Core Privacy Principles

  • Local-only storage: All data is stored exclusively on the user’s device.
  • No data collection: We do not collect any personal information.
  • No data transmission: No data is transmitted to any server.
  • User control: Users may export or delete their data at any time.

Data Stored on Device

All information entered in the app, including financial plans, goals, monthly records, and app preferences, is stored locally using iOS system security mechanisms and protected by device authentication.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identity information
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Payment, banking, or financial account information

Third-Party Services

Road2Wealthy does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, cloud storage, or artificial intelligence services.

Policy Updates

Any significant changes to this privacy policy will be communicated through app update notes or in-app notifications.

核心隐私原则

  • 完全本地存储：所有数据仅保存在您的设备上。

您在应用中输入的所有信息，包括财务计划、目标设定、月度记录及应用偏好， 均存储于本地设备，并受 iOS 系统安全机制及设备验证保护。

我们不收集的信息

  • 个人身份信息
  • 联系方式
  • 位置信息
  • 设备标识符或广告 ID
  • 银行账户或支付信息

第三方服务

慢慢富有 不集成任何第三方分析、广告、云存储或人工智能服务。

隐私政策更新
