This Privacy Policy applies only to the iOS app “Road2Wealthy”.

English Version

Introduction

Road2Wealthy is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Core Privacy Principles

  • Local-only storage: All data is stored only on your device.
  • Zero data collection: We do not collect any personal information.
  • No network transmission: Data is not uploaded to any server.
  • Full user control: You can export or delete your data at any time.

Local Data Storage

All data in Road2Wealthy is stored only on your local device, including:

  • Financial plans and goal settings
  • Monthly contribution records
  • App settings and preferences

This data is protected by iOS system security mechanisms and guarded by your device passcode and biometric authentication.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identity information (e.g., legal name, government ID)
  • Contact information (e.g., phone number)
  • Location and geographic data
  • Device identifiers and advertising IDs
  • Contacts and social relationships
  • Usage data from other apps
  • Any form of biometric data
  • Bank account or payment information

Scope of Data Usage

The financial data you enter is used only for the following purposes on your device:

  • Financial calculations and demonstrations
  • Displaying planning results and charts
  • Tracking goal progress
  • Generating monthly and historical reports

Third-Party Services

Road2Wealthy is a fully independent app:

  • No third-party analytics SDKs
  • No advertising or tracking services
  • No connections to social media platforms
  • No cloud storage services
  • No external APIs used for data processing

We are committed to maintaining the app’s independence and privacy-first approach.

Privacy Policy Updates

If we make significant updates to this privacy policy:

  • We will clearly notify you in the app update notes
  • Major changes will be highlighted within the app
  • You may choose whether to accept the updated policy

引言

核心隐私原则
